Rules

  • One account per team. No team size limit, but we may begin to judge you if you get too far into the double digits, and we will be very disapproving if you get into triple digits or higher.
  • Ties will be broken by the timestamp of the last submitted flag (the earlier the better).
  • Find the flag. The flag is always in the form tpctf{flag} or TPCTF{flag} unless specified otherwise.
  • Don't share flags with others outside of your team. Why would you do that?
  • Don't attack our websites. If you find a vulnerability that you believe was unintended, PM us.
  • Contact us through email or on IRC with the channel #tpctf. Mods are kmh11, kmh12, kmh13, kmh14, krnh11, lamchcl, and ctfhaxor. Bots include krnh12, flagbot, flagbotv3, flagbotbot, flagbotv3botv2, and Capt4inAmer1ca.
  • Have fun!
